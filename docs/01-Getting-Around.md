# 🗺 Getting Around the App

Welcome! If you are new to the app, don't worry—it is designed to be simple and very easy to use. 

## The Main Screen
When you open the app, you will see two main areas on your screen:
1. **The Sidebar (Left Side):** This is your main menu. You can think of it like the table of contents in a book. Click on any item or folder here to switch to that page. 
2. **The Main Workspace (Right Side):** This is where you actually write, read, and do your work. When you click something in the sidebar, it opens up here in the middle.

## How to Find Things
- Notice the **Search** option near the top of the sidebar. If you ever lose a page or a note, just type a word you remember from it, and it will pop right up!
- To hide the sidebar and focus entirely on your writing, look for a small menu button near the top left (it usually looks like three horizontal lines or an arrow). Clicking it will tuck the sidebar away to give you more reading room.

Take a moment to click around. **You cannot break anything just by clicking**, so feel free to explore!
