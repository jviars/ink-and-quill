# ↕️ Moving Things Around (Reordering)

Sometimes you write something and later decide it should be higher up or lower down on the page. The good news is you do not need to delete it and type it all over again! You can simply move it.

## How to "Drag and Drop"
1. **Hover your mouse pointer** over the sentence, list item, or block of text that you want to move.
2. Look closely at the very left edge of the text. You will notice a small icon appear—it usually looks like **six little dots** in a rectangle. (Some people call this a "grip handle").
3. **Click and hold** your left mouse button on those six dots. Keep holding the button down!
4. While still holding the button down, **drag your mouse** up or down. You will see the item move along with your mouse pointer.
5. Once the item is hovering exactly where you want it to go, simply **let go** of the mouse button.

That is it! It drops right into place. You can use this exact same "click, hold, and drag" motion to reorder To-Do list items, paragraphs, or folders in the sidebar.
