# ✨ The Magic "/" Key (Slash Commands)

We have a special shortcut that makes writing and adding things to your page incredibly fast. You don't even need to use your mouse! We call this feature **Slash Commands**.

## What is a Slash Command?
Whenever you are typing on a page, try pressing the **slash key (`/`)** on your keyboard. It's the same key as the question mark (`?`), usually near the bottom right of the keyboard letters.

As soon as you type `/`, a little menu list will automatically pop up on your screen.

## How to use it
1. Click on an empty line in your document and type `/`.
2. A list of options will appear (like adding a big Title, a To-Do List box, or a Bulleted List).
3. You can either use your mouse to click what you want from the list, OR you can keep typing to narrow the list down. For example, typing `/todo` will highlight the To-Do List option.
4. Press the **Enter** (or Return) key on your keyboard to confirm your choice.

**Things to try next time you are writing:**
- Type `/h1` and press Enter to make a BIG, bold title.
- Type `/bullet` and press Enter to start a bulleted list.
- Type `/check` to add a clickable checkbox.

It might feel a little unusual at first, but once you get the hang of it, you'll feel like a computer wizard!
