# ⚙️ Settings Explained

The Settings menu is a safe space where you can tweak the app to make it work best for you. You can usually find the Settings menu by looking for a **Gear Icon**, generally located at the bottom of the sidebar list on the left.

Here is what you will typically find inside, and what it all means:

## 🌙 Appearance (Dark Mode vs Light Mode)
Do bright, glowing screens hurt your eyes at night? You can actually change the overall colors of the app!
- **Light Mode:** The background is white and the text is black. This looks just like reading a piece of physical paper.
- **Dark Mode:** The background changes to a dark gray or black, and the text turns white. This is much gentler on your eyes in dark or dimly lit rooms.
- **System or Auto:** The app will automatically switch between Light and Dark depending on what your computer is set to do throughout the day.

## 👤 Profile & Account
This section is all about you. Here you can update your name, fix your email address if it is wrong, or update your password. 

## 🔔 Notifications
Decide if you want the app to alert or ding you when certain things happen. If you find pop-ups or badges distracting, this is the area where you can turn them off for peace and quiet.

---
*Remember: You don't HAVE to change any settings if you do not want to! The app is completely ready to go and works perfectly well exactly as it comes.*
